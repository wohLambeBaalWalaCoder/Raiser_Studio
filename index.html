What We Teach

Dance Styles

Bollywood Dance

A vibrant and energetic dance style inspired by Indian cinema. A beautiful fusion of classical Indian dance forms, folk styles, and modern influences like hip-hop and contemporary.

Hip-Hop Dance

A powerful, high-energy street dance style known for its freedom, attitude, rhythm, and self-expression. Express your personality, confidence, and creativity through strong movements.

Semi-Classical

A graceful fusion of traditional Indian classical dance forms and modern expressions. Combines beauty, discipline, and storytelling of classical dance with contemporary flow.

Contemporary

A powerful and expressive dance style that blends classical, modern, and lyrical movement. Focuses on emotion, storytelling, fluidity, and freedom of expression.
